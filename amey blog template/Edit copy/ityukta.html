Ityukta

  • May 05, 2018
  • In Projects

Ityukta is the a Newsletter that the department of ISE, RNS Institute of Technology releases every year. It is a consolidation of all the event that have taken place under the department, and also comprising the news on latest technologies and advancements in the field of Computer Science, written by the students. I was given the opportunity to design the newsletter for the year 2018, and I feel that I delivered to the expectations. I also received a certificate for the same.
